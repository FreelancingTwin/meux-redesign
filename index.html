The Meux Home Meuseum

In 1888, Dr. Thomas Richard Meux’s 16-room Victorian mansion became a reality.

It is as impressive today as it was over a century ago.

As our guest, you’re invited to enjoy a guided tour of the house, as well as stroll the beautiful garden.

Tours take 45 minutes to 1 hour, depending on your pace. You’ll see all the best in architecture, artwork, and much more from the Victorian Age.

Admission: Adults $10.00 Children (Ages 6-17) $7.00 Children 5 and under are free. Masks not required but encouraged when inside the museum.
